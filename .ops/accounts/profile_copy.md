## Profile Image Prompt

Create a clean square profile image for a brand called WorthScan. Use a simple
scanner-frame mark around a price tag, with the word WorthScan readable at small
sizes. Style should feel practical, modern, and trustworthy for resale checks,
not luxury finance. Use high contrast, a white or near-white background, dark
charcoal text, and one accent color in green or blue. Avoid tiny text, currency
symbols that imply guaranteed value, platform logos, seller photos, and private
listing details.

## Banner Prompt

Create a YouTube channel banner for WorthScan. Show a tidy desktop with three
generic resale items: a commuter bike crop, an electric scooter handlebar, and a
mini fridge detail, arranged like a manual comparison board. Add subtle visual
elements for comps, condition checks, and a value range. Leave safe empty space
in the center for channel text. Style should be crisp, useful, and operator-led,
with no platform logos, no real seller information, and no promise of guaranteed
appraisals.

## Link Destination Rules

- Use only an operator-approved public destination.
- Do not add unpublished forms or tracking tools without review.
- Do not request platform passwords, recovery codes, or identity documents.
- Do not collect seller contact details beyond what the human operator has
  approved for the intake workflow.

## Pinned Intro Post Direction

Create a manual intro post that explains the repeatable format:

- show the item and asking price
