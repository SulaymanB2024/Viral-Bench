# Blocked Browser Tasks

- Scraping TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, or Creative Center at scale.
- Calling hidden endpoints or replaying app requests.
- Automating account creation, login, CAPTCHA, phone verification, email verification, or payment setup.
- Posting, scheduling, commenting, liking, following, or messaging through social accounts.
- Collecting private account data, cookies, session tokens, raw credentials, seller contact details, or private messages.
- Bypassing rate limits, geo gates, paywalls, access controls, or platform terms gates.
- Treating missing browser/provider coverage as evidence that a trend does not exist.

If a task touches a blocked action, stop and ask for a narrower approved workflow.
