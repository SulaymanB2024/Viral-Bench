## Shared Posting Rules

- Use only approved local item visuals.
- Remove seller names, addresses, payment info, private messages, and private
  account identifiers.
- Verify comps manually on the posting day.
- Keep valuation wording range-based.
- Include an estimate disclaimer in the caption or first comment.
- Post manually in the official platform app or website.
- Create the metrics record only after the post is live.
