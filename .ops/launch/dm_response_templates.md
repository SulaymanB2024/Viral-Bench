# WorthScan First DM Response Templates

Use these as manual response drafts only. Do not automate DMs or replies.

## Listing Scan Request

Thanks for sending it. I can do a WorthScan-style read if you send the asking
price, item photos or listing screenshots, city or campus area, known
model/specs, and any defects or missing accessories.

I will give a resale estimate range, not a guaranteed appraisal.

## Quick Intake

Send:

- Asking price
- Item photos or screenshots
- City or campus area
- Known model/specs
- Defects, missing parts, or included accessories

Private seller details should be removed before anything becomes public.

## Missing Comps

I need a few comparable listings before giving a useful range. If you can send
two or three similar local listings, I can compare model, condition, accessories,
and pickup hassle.

## Exact Value Boundary

I cannot call an exact value from one listing photo. The useful answer is a
range with confidence, comps, value drivers, and risk flags.

## Not Buying Or Brokering

WorthScan is not buying the item, brokering the sale, or guaranteeing a resale
price. It is content and decision-support, not an official appraisal.

## Permission To Feature

If you want this turned into a public post, confirm that the screenshots can be
used after private info is removed. A human still reviews everything before
posting.

## Soft Decline

I cannot review this one from the current info. WorthScan needs visible item
details, an asking price, and comparable local listings before giving a range.

## Follow-Up CTA

If you want another item checked, send the listing and write `scan` at the top
so it is easy to spot.
