# WorthScan Pinned Comment Templates

Use one pinned comment per manual post. Keep it short and platform-native.

## General Scan Prompt

Comment `scan` with the next listing you want reviewed. WorthScan gives an
estimate range, not a guaranteed appraisal.

## Comps Prompt

Want a better range? Drop the item, asking price, and two local comps.

## Safety Boundary

Remove seller names, addresses, payment info, and private messages before
sending anything for a public review.

## Bike Post

For bikes, send frame brand, size, tire/brake condition, asking price, and three
local comps.

## Scooter Post

For scooters, send model, mileage, charger proof, battery clues, asking price,
and three local comps.

## Mini Fridge Post

For mini fridges, send size, brand, freezer compartment, cleanliness notes,
pickup area, and three local comps.
