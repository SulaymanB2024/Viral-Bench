# Creative Center Research Capture Prompt

Use this as a checklist for manual or browser-assisted Creative Center research.

Capture:

- Source URL and source name.
- Capture timestamp.
- Niche and platform.
- Observed format.
- Visible metrics exactly as shown.
- Hook, caption, or visible text.
- Visual notes.
- Why it may work.
- Remake notes.
- Evidence notes.
- Human review status.

Do not scrape, bypass gates, automate login, or collect hidden/private data. Keep the output in the browser capture schema and ingest only after human approval.
