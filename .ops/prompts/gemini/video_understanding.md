# Gemini Video Understanding Prompt

Provider mode: dry-run scaffold.

Use this prompt only for operator-approved video or screenshot assets. Do not fetch TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, or Creative Center media directly.

Task:

Summarize the visible format, hook, pacing, proof structure, and remake opportunities from the provided local media asset.

Output:

- Observed format.
- Hook and first-frame notes.
- Visible proof steps.
- Remake notes for the current job.
- Evidence limitations.

Rules:

- Treat missing audio, cropped UI, blurred metrics, or unclear source context as limitations.
- Do not infer private analytics or account ownership.
- Do not turn provider gaps into trend claims.
