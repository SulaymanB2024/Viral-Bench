# Social Account Setup

Prepare account materials only. Account creation and verification stay manual.

## Allowed

- Draft handle ideas, profile copy, launch checklists, and public metadata.
- Update `.ops/accounts/` with non-secret operator notes.
- Prepare posting package notes for the human account owner.

## Not Allowed

- Automated account creation.
- Automated login, CAPTCHA, phone, age, or identity verification.
- Password, token, cookie, session, recovery-code, or phone-number storage in the
  repo.
- Auto-posting or scheduling content without an explicit reviewed workflow.

## Files

- `.ops/accounts/socials.md`
- `.ops/accounts/account_setup_checklist.md`
- `.ops/accounts/handle_ideas.md`
- `.ops/accounts/profile_copy.md`
- `.ops/accounts/launch_checklist.md`
